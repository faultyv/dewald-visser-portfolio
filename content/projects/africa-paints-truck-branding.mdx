## The brief

Africa Paints, a paint manufacturer trading since 1990, needed its delivery fleet to double as a moving billboard for the brand and its "Platinum Series" product range.

## What I did

Designed the full tarpaulin livery: the "africa paints" hexagon mark, the "The Future...Is In Colour" campaign line and a product shelf for the Platinum Series across the box-body panels, plus a dedicated rear-door layout with the campaign tagline and contact details.

## The reach

Every panel was treated as its own placement, so the branding reads correctly whether the truck is parked, reversing or passing on the road.
