## The brief

Grow car rentals and replace a manual, spreadsheet-driven booking process that was losing track of clients, payments, fines and vehicle maintenance.

## What I did

Ran organic and sponsored search, paid social and email campaigns with proper budget planning. Built a custom WordPress appointment system that tracked every client, payment, fine and service interval. Produced animated video and image adverts to carry the campaigns.

## The system

The booking system became the operational backbone of the business — replacing scattered spreadsheets with a single source of truth for every vehicle, client and payment, while the marketing side kept new bookings flowing into it.
