## The brief

Grow car rentals while replacing a manual, spreadsheet-driven booking process that was losing track of clients, payments, fines and vehicle maintenance.

## What I did

Ran organic search, sponsored search, paid social and email campaigns with budget planning behind them. Built a custom WordPress appointment system that tracked every client, payment, fine and service interval. Produced animated video and image adverts to carry the campaigns.

## The system

The booking system became the operational backbone of the business, replacing scattered spreadsheets with a single source of truth for every vehicle, client and payment while the marketing kept new bookings flowing into it. The site is still live today, covering car rentals, bakkie and truck hire, and seasonal specials.
