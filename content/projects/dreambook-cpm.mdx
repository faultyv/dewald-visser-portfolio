## The brief

Clinton Palframan Ministries, based in Hillcrest, needed a single site to carry two published works — The Dreambook and Living From The Inside Out — plus its app, resources and products, under one coherent identity. Pastor Clinton Palframan is also Director of Mediatrade and Dean of Joseph Business School, and sits on the boards of JBSA and Bill Winston Ministries — so the identity had to read with equal weight across ministry, education and enterprise audiences.

## What I did

Designed the website end-to-end (home, about, resources, products, contact) and the supporting brand mark for The Dreambook: a lettering treatment where each letter is filled with imagery tied to the book's themes — night sky, a crown and sword, a child reaching for flight, mountains and sea, fire, a seedling, a child looking up, an open hand. Built out the App Store / Google Play download section and the live-event sign-up flow used for the Dreambook Immersion Day webinars.

## The structure

Two brand strands, one site: The Dreambook ("Unlocking the Kingdom Within You") and Living From The Inside Out ("Living a Victorious and Overcoming Life") sit as distinct sections under the Clinton Palframan Ministries identity, sharing the same navigation, product shelf and contact flow.

## The partnership

This was never a one-off brief. My own client portfolio was acquired by Mediatrade, and I took a 20% stake in the Dreambook project — the Director wanted me focused full-time across his ventures. So the brand, the website and the digital product were built and carried as a partner with skin in the game, not as a vendor delivering a job.
