## The brief

A Hillcrest-based ministry organisation needed a single site to carry two published works — The Dreambook and Living From The Inside Out — plus its app, resources and products, under one coherent identity. The work sat at the intersection of ministry, education and enterprise audiences, so the identity needed to feel devotional, credible and commercially presentable at the same time.

## What I did

Designed the website end-to-end (home, about, resources, products, contact) and the supporting brand mark for The Dreambook: a lettering treatment where each letter is filled with imagery tied to the book's themes — night sky, a crown and sword, a child reaching for flight, mountains and sea, fire, a seedling, a child looking up, an open hand. Built out the App Store / Google Play download section and the live-event sign-up flow used for the Dreambook Immersion Day webinars.

## The structure

Two brand strands, one site: The Dreambook ("Unlocking the Kingdom Within You") and Living From The Inside Out ("Living a Victorious and Overcoming Life") sit as distinct sections under one ministry identity, sharing the same navigation, product shelf and contact flow.

## The partnership

This was not a detached vendor brief. It became part of a wider transition where my agency client portfolio moved into a production and education business group, and The Dreambook became one of the flagship web and brand systems I carried inside that context.
