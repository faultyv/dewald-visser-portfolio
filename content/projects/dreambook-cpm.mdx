## The brief

Clinton Palframan Ministries, based in Hillcrest, needed a single site to carry two published works — The Dreambook and Living From The Inside Out — plus its app, resources and products, under one coherent identity.

## What I did

Designed the website end-to-end (home, about, resources, products, contact) and the supporting brand mark for The Dreambook: a lettering treatment where each letter is filled with imagery tied to the book's themes — night sky, a crown and sword, a child reaching for flight, mountains and sea, fire, a seedling, a child looking up, an open hand. Built out the App Store / Google Play download section and the live-event sign-up flow used for the Dreambook Immersion Day webinars.

## The structure

Two brand strands, one site: The Dreambook ("Unlocking the Kingdom Within You") and Living From The Inside Out ("Living a Victorious and Overcoming Life") sit as distinct sections under the Clinton Palframan Ministries identity, sharing the same navigation, product shelf and contact flow.
