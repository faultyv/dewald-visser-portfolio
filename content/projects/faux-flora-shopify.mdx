## The pressure

When hard lockdown hit, the studio needed cash flow fast. Physical design and installation work stopped overnight. The goal: create a revenue line that worked with zero foot traffic.

## What I built

A full Shopify store — fauxflora.co.za — with a drop-ship supply model, product design and reporting, covering the full customer path: home, shop, projects gallery and contact, plus a downloadable catalogue. Launched quickly and run end-to-end. It kept money moving when it mattered most.

## The outcome

The store stood in for the business's entire physical offering for the duration of lockdown, protecting both cash flow and the jobs that depended on it.
