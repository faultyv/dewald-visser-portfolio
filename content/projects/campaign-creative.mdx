## The work

Campaign creative at scale — animated adverts, booklets and email creative built to sell, across a high-volume education business.

## The approach

Designed templates, booklets, animated video adverts and email campaign creative. Paired the design with sales, advising and data analytics so the creative was tied to outcomes, not just output.

## The brands

Started on the launch team for Educor's online brands (Damelin and CityVarsity), then moved onto the group's first international brand, LCIBS (London College of International Business Studies), as an international admissions consultant — campaign creative had to work across very different audiences within the same group.
