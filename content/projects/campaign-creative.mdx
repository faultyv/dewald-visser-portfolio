## The work

Campaign creative and admissions support across online education brands: animated adverts, booklets and email creative built to support enrolment, programme promotion and student decision-making.

## The approach

Designed templates, booklets, animated video adverts and email campaign creative. Paired the design work with advising, CRM discipline and data analytics so the creative was tied to enrolment outcomes, not just output.

## The brands

Started on the launch team for Damelin Online and CityVarsity online activity, then moved onto LCIBS (London College of International Business Studies) as an international admissions consultant. The work had to flex across local online education, international admissions and early conversations about preparing teams for AI-led change, including programme-launch activity under former Apple executive Brent Kilpatrick before AI became mainstream business language.
