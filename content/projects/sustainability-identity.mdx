## The brief

An international sustainable-development initiative, run out of Cambridge University, needed a visual identity to launch under.

## What I did

Designed the logo and a set of supporting mockups so the initiative had a complete, presentable identity ready for its launch materials.

## The constraint

Working internationally and remotely, on a project with a development and academic audience rather than a commercial one — the mark had to read as credible and serious first.
