## The brief

An international sustainable-development initiative, run out of Cambridge University, needed a visual identity to launch under the theme "Connecting Water to Global Citizenship through Education for Sustainable Development."

## What I did

Designed the logo and a set of supporting mockups so the initiative had a complete, presentable identity ready for launch materials. The mark folds three figures reaching upward into a globe held in an open hand - water, citizenship and education in one symbol - built to scale from a favicon to a conference banner.

## The constraint

Working internationally and remotely, for a development and academic audience rather than a commercial one, meant the mark had to read as credible and serious first.
