## The client

Dynamic Automation is a family-run South African manufacturer and supplier of poultry-farming equipment — broiler, drinking, feeding, ventilation and battery-cage systems — and one of the manufacturing clients I supported through my own agency.

## What I did

Handled the marketing, branding and signage, and built and maintained the website that presents the full equipment range to commercial poultry farmers. The site stayed current with products, projects and content as the catalogue grew.

## The quote tool

The standout piece: turning a tangled engineering quoting spreadsheet into a web-based quote tool. I translated complex workbook logic — modules, options and quote intelligence — into a guided, self-serve quoting flow deployed as a working prototype. The kind of systems work that takes a business off email-and-Excel and onto something repeatable.
