## The brief

Joseph Business School Africa is the African hub of the global Joseph Business School network - the entrepreneurship school founded under Bill Winston Ministries in Chicago - equipping entrepreneurs using practical and biblical principles through a nine-month, part-time programme. The work was bigger than media output: make the Africa programme easier to market, easier to teach, easier to watch, easier to sell and easier to repeat.

## What I did

Ran business-programme, multimedia and live-production support for the school end to end. Livestreamed sessions to audiences of 500+, ran masterclasses, coordinated speakers, content and run of show, and recorded and edited the learning material. Built the sales funnels that filled the programmes, designed the campaigns and ads that promoted them, and helped turn the offer into a more self-sustaining content and enrolment model. The work connected public video proof, learning content, web/LMS surfaces, social/email creative, Canva plus ChatGPT workflows and the front-end that presents it all.

## Why it matters to me

This is the kind of work I want more of: a clear mission, real learners, ambitious commercial pressure and a remit that runs from the idea through the campaign to the system that delivers it.
