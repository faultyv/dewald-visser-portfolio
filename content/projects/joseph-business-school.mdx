## The brief

Joseph Business School Africa is the African hub of the global Joseph Business School network — the entrepreneurship school founded under Bill Winston Ministries in Chicago — equipping entrepreneurs using practical and biblical principles through a nine-month, part-time programme. The work: produce the multimedia, design and web that carry that message and present the learning.

## What I did

Multimedia specialist across the full stack of content — video and motion, visual design, learning material, and the front-end that presents it. The job was to make a values-led education brand feel as considered online as it is in the room.

## Why it matters to me

This is the kind of work I want more of: a clear mission, real learners, and a remit that runs from the idea through the design to the system that delivers it.
