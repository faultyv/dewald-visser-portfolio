## How it came together

After my agency client portfolio moved into Mediatrade's production environment, I came on as a production creative across retail and house-brand work - alongside Joseph Business School Africa programme, media and growth-systems work in the same wider group. It put a roster of major retailers in front of me to turn into press-ready output at scale.

## The work

Production graphic design for retail at scale: point-of-sale, floor-standing units (FSUs), Christmas crackers and Eveready blister-card artwork for some of South Africa's biggest house brands — Pick n Pay, Game, Makro, Mr Price and more — alongside licensed and stationery brands like Disney, Croxley, Staedtler and Parker.

## Artworking & DTP

The craft here is precision: print-ready DTP, clean artworking, and laying every job up so it actually runs on press. Christmas crackers for Pick n Pay, Game, Mr Price and Makro; blister cards for Eveready; display and shelf-edge POS across the retail floor.

## Scheming, nesting & less waste

A lot of the value is invisible on the shelf: scheming and nesting designs for repro so that material is used efficiently and waste is kept to a minimum. Good production design saves the client money on every run, not just the first one.
