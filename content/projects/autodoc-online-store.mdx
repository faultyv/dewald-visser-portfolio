## The brief

Autodoc Diagnostics needed an online store to sell direct, and a brand mark that looked the part once it launched.

## What I did

Built the online store end-to-end, wrote the site and product copy, and sourced the suppliers behind the catalogue. Redesigned the company logo in Illustrator and InDesign — the rebrand kept the auto-repair shorthand (the car-sweep line, the bold "AutoDoc" wordmark) but gave it the contrast and weight to hold up on a storefront, a van, or a business card.

## The role

A front-end-and-brand role in one — the store had to function and convert, and the identity carrying it had to hold up next to it.
