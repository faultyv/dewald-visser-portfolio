## The venture

Sun Paper & Coatings is my founder/operator build: a business created around self-adhesive paper, label stock and coating-material supply. The work is not just a logo or a landing page; it is the early operating layer of the company — positioning, product language, supplier presentation, sales collateral and the repeatable assets needed to make the venture look credible while it is still being assembled.

## What I built

Defined the brand direction, prepared the visual identity for practical B2B use, and started shaping the sales materials around how printers, converters and buyers actually evaluate stock: material type, finish, reliability, supply route and responsiveness. The same system carries through the brochure, product visuals, supply graphics and email presentation.

## The operating layer

The goal is to make the business easier to sell and easier to run at the same time. That means clear product naming, usable visuals, contact-ready collateral, and brand applications that can move from a PDF to an email signature to a supplier conversation without feeling patched together.

## Why it belongs here

This page shows the portfolio moving from client delivery into ownership. The same blend of brand, web, marketing and systems thinking now has to support my own venture — with the extra pressure that the decisions are not theoretical. They affect the business I am building.
