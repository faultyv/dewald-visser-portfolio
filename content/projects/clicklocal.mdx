## The venture

clicklocal was my own digital and design agency for local South African businesses. The pitch was simple: one membership, uncapped creative — "like having your own butler". Through the **Premier Club** service suite, members got graphic design, social media, websites, SEO, print and signage handled end to end, instead of juggling freelancers.

## What members got

Uncapped graphic design — logos and corporate identity, stationery, brochures, product and label design — plus weekly social media across Facebook, Instagram and LinkedIn with monthly reporting, print and billboard ad design, SEO and Google Business setup, and a free-forever business website with hosting. Paid advertising, photography and copywriting sat on top as optional services, all on month-to-month or contract packages.

## The clients & the exit

I ran the brand, the offer and the client base behind Africa Paints, Solid Doors, Alif Doors, Natal Associated Chemicals and Dynamic Automation — across brand, web, campaigns, signage and production. The client portfolio was later acquired and serviced through Mediatrade, the transition that moved me full-time onto the Director's ventures.
