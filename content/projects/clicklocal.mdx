## The venture

clicklocal.co.za was shaped as a local-business growth platform: part brand, part content engine and part operating system. The public presence had to make practical services feel packaged, understandable and ready to buy, from website design to social creative, signage, labels and print production.

## The ownership

Director-level ownership of the whole thing: positioning, visual language, service packaging, social content, platform structure and the workflows behind delivery. The Instagram feed became evidence of range, showing how one brand could move across digital, print and outdoor formats while still feeling like one system.
